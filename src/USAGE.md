# LiveToon: Animated Wallpapers

To set these animated backgrounds as your Mac desktop:

1. Create your animated scene in this app, tweak parameters until you love it.
2. Click "**Get Wallpaper URL**" to copy your unique generated URL.
3. Download **[Plash](https://sindresorhus.com/plash)**, an amazing open-source tool for macOS.
4. Open Plash on your Mac, click "**Add Website...**", and paste the URL.
5. Watch your desktop come to life!
